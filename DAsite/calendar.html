    • What Calendars?

    At CCI, there are 4 calendars you need to keep track of:

    • Academic year calendar
    • CCI information events calendar
    • Steinbright's career events calendar
    • Your own!
    Drexel uses Outlook for email and calendars. Use your Outlook to organize all these calendars in one place!

    Academic Year Calendar

    The Drexel Academic year calendar has important information like:

    • Quarter start and end dates
    • Drop/Add period dates
    • deadlines to submit Application for Degree
    • University holidays

    CCI Information Events Calendar

    The CCI information events calendar shows the upcoming Graduate Q&A sessions and CCI degree information sessions.

    You can register for the online events through that calendar.

    The information events are a great way to learn about your major requirements, meet CCI faculty and DAs, and ask questions!

    Steinbright's Career Events Calendar

    Visit the Steinbright Career Development Center's event calendar to see all upcoming events, including resume workshops, co-op prep sessions, and career fairs.
