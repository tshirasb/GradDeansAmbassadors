    • Advisor FAQs

    Who is my advisor?

    Go to Drexel Connect > DrexelOne > Academics. Your academic advisor's name and email are under the "Academic Advisor" section.

    You can also find a list of all CCI graduate advisors here.

    How should I get in touch?

    Email is the preferred method. The advisors reply promptly during work hours.

    Advisor Tips

    How do you get the most out of CCI advising? Here's some tips:

    • Contact your advisor early.
      • Peak times, like the week of registration, can be hectic. Set up a meeting with your advisor in advance to beat the rush.
    • Don't repeatedly email.
      • Your advisor works hard to reply to all emails within a couple work days. Respect them by sending one email at a time.
    • Use DrexelOne and Degreeworks
      • Keep track of your course requirements, registration, and future schedules. Be familiar with these vital tools!

    Degreeworks

    What's Degreeworks?

    Degreeworks shows you every course you've taken, how the course counts towards your degree, your final grades, and how your planned courses will help you complete your degree. It's a powerful tool!

    Finding Degreeworks

    Drexel Connect > DrexelOne > Academics > Registration. Click Degreeworks Graduation Requirements.

    If nothing opens at first, allow pop-ups on that page.
