    • Who are the DAs?

    Drexel's Graduate Dean's Ambassadors (DAs) are current graduate students in CCI. Their job is to welcome new students by sharing their experiences and answering questions.

    Contacting DAs

    You can talk to a DA on the CCI official Discord by going to "Graduate Programs" > ask-a-graduate-peer-mentor and sending a message. There's always a DA available to chat!

    You can also contact DAs individually by email. Current DAs are listed here on the CCI website with their email addresses.
